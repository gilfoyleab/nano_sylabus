# Product Overview

## What Nano Syllabus is

Nano Syllabus is an AI-powered EdTech platform for Nepal. It is designed as a bilingual study companion that gives students personalized, curriculum-aligned help through a familiar chat interface.

The key differentiator is not just "AI chat." The product is meant to feel academically local:

- answers aligned to Nepal-specific curricula such as NEB, TU, PU, KU, and CTEVT
- support for English and Roman Nepali
- personalization using the student's academic context
- revision workflows built around exam preparation

## Vision

To become Nepal's most trusted AI study companion by making quality, personalized education accessible regardless of language background or access to private tuition.

## Core value proposition

For students:

- ask questions the way they naturally think
- get simpler, syllabus-aware explanations
- reduce friction caused by English-only study tools
- turn useful answers into saved revision material

For the business:

- build a defensible, Nepal-specific academic knowledge layer
- monetize through recurring subscription plans
- create long-term retention through study and revision habits

## Primary users

### Students

Students from Class 9 through bachelor's level are the main users. They use the app daily for doubt-solving, concept clarity, and revision.

### Secondary users

- teachers and tutors who may recommend the platform
- parents who influence adoption and trust
- admins and internal operators who manage content and subscriptions

## Representative personas

### Aarav

- Class 11 Science student

### Sita

- BBS Year 1 student
- wants step-by-step explanations
- prefers simpler language and comfort reading in Roman Nepali

## Product pillars

### 1. Curriculum alignment

The product must be grounded in actual Nepali syllabi, not generic internet explanations.

### 2. Language accessibility

Roman Nepali support is central, not optional polish.

### 3. Personalization

The student profile should shape the difficulty, tone, and context of explanations.

### 4. Retention through study workflows

Chat is the entry point, but notes and revision mode are what should turn usage into habit.

### 5. Trust and academic reliability

Students should feel this tool is relevant, safe, and locally useful.

## Product surfaces

The full product vision includes:

- student-facing web app / PWA
- marketing website
- admin panel
- backend services
- AI and knowledge-base pipeline
